Who We Are

Baxter Academy for Technology and Science is a non-profit, public charter school located in Portland, Maine. Our mission is to provide a rigorous and engaging education in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) to students in grades 9-12.

Our teachers are experts in their fields and are committed to inspiring students to pursue their passions and achieve their goals. We believe that learning should be fun and challenging, and that every student has the potential to make a difference in the world.

Our History

Baxter Academy was founded in 2013 by a group of educators and community members who saw the need for a high-quality STEM education program in Maine. Since then, we have grown to become one of the leading STEM schools in the region, with a track record of academic excellence and student success.
